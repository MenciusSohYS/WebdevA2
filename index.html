SCIENCE FICTION

Is a Genre fiction based on imagined future scientific or technological advances and major social or environmental changes, frequently portraying space or time travel and life on other planets. Some Genres portray hope, like Dune or The Culture while others portray nilhilism and impending doom like in Hyperion or Cthuhlu Mythos. Some of my favourites would include, Warhammer 40k, Xeelee Sequence, Rememberance of Earth's past, Manifold Triology, Children of Time, Cowboy Bebop and Neon Genesis Evangelion
